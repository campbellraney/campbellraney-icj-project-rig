Atlas

Meet Atlas — a golden-hearted senior with a splash of charm! This large, golden and black mixed breed is friendly, smart, and house-trained. Atlas enjoys playing fetch, tug-of-war, and splashing in the pool. He knows commands like "sit," "go to pillow," "leave it," and "come" (unless a squirrel distracts him). Atlas prefers a home without other dogs, cats, or children. His adoption fee has been covered by an Adoption Angel, so he's ready to find his forever home.​ Find him at Williamson County Regional Animal Shelter
