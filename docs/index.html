DIAMONDS IN THE RUFF

Older Dogs, New Leashes on Life.

Because Every Senior Dog Deserves a Forever Home

At Diamonds in the Ruff, we’re on a mission to prove that old dogs can learn new tricks — like how it feels to be truly loved. We specialize in helping kind-hearted humans find their perfect match in wise, wonderful, and waggy-tailed senior dogs.

These golden oldies may have a little gray on their muzzle, but they’ve got endless love to give. Our mission is to make their final chapters the happiest — full of belly rubs, long naps, and lots of love.
